How It Works

Three layers of protection

Iron Gate sits between your team and AI tools, analyzing every prompt in real time before it leaves your organization.

1

Detect

Chrome extension monitors AI tool usage in real time. Every prompt is analyzed for PII, trade secrets, and privileged content before it leaves the browser.

2

Protect

Sensitive entities are pseudonymized with realistic fakes. "John Smith" becomes "James Mitchell" -- not [REDACTED]. AI answers stay useful.

3

Control

Firm-wide dashboard shows risk analytics, compliance status, and usage trends. Know exactly what your team is sharing with AI tools.

Key Features

Built for regulated industries

Not just another DLP tool. Iron Gate understands the difference between generic questions and confidential client work.

EL

Executive Lens

Understands what IS the IP, not just PII. A chemical formula has no names or SSNs -- but your CEO would never approve sharing it with ChatGPT.

SP

Smart Pseudonymization

Replaces "John Smith" with "James Mitchell", not [REDACTED]. AI models receive realistic data and return useful answers. Real identities never leave your network.

3R

Three Routing Modes

Passthrough for safe queries. Pseudonymized proxy for PII-containing prompts. Private LLM routing when the content itself is the secret.

IA

Industry Awareness

Legal, Healthcare, Finance, Manufacturing -- context-specific rules for each industry. Knows that attorney-client privilege is different from HIPAA compliance.

AE

AES-256-GCM Encryption

Per-firm encryption keys derived via PBKDF2. Zero plaintext at rest. Every audit log entry is encrypted with a unique initialization vector.

RT

Real-Time Dashboard

Firm-wide analytics, risk trends, and compliance reports. See who is using what AI tools, what they are sharing, and where the risks are -- in real time.

Compliance & Security

Built with compliance in mind

Iron Gate helps professional services firms monitor and control data sharing when adopting AI tools.

1.6

ABA Model Rule 1.6

Client Confidentiality. Requires lawyers to make "reasonable efforts" to prevent unauthorized disclosure of client information to third parties, including AI services.

512

ABA Formal Opinion 512

AI and Confidentiality. Addresses the specific obligations lawyers have when using generative AI tools that may process or retain client data.

H

HIPAA-Aware Detection

Detects Protected Health Information patterns and routes flagged content to private infrastructure. Helps limit PHI exposure to external AI services.

DM

Data Minimization

No raw prompt text is stored. Pseudonym mappings are encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM. Self-hostable deployment means your data never leaves your infrastructure.
